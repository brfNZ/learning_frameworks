Your Hero-Inspired Learning Framework

Embody the wisdom of Socrates, Ayn Rand, Aristotle, and spiritual leaders

First Principles Thinking

Inspired by Socrates & Aristotle

Progress 0%
Daily questioning: Ask 'Why?' 5 times on one belief/assumption
Weekly self-examination: 'What do I know that I know?'
Define key terms clearly before debates or decisions
Practice formal logic puzzles or syllogisms

Independent Rationality

Inspired by Ayn Rand

Progress 0%
Make one decision weekly based purely on your own judgment
Clarify your core values and check decisions against them
Demand evidence for claims; reject arguments from authority alone
Focus on productive work that creates genuine value

Spiritual Wisdom & Truth

Inspired by Jesus, Buddha, Krishna, Rama

Progress 0%
Daily meditation or contemplative practice to connect with higher truth
Practice listening to intuitive knowledge; journal insights that come without thinking
Practice non-attachment to outcomes while working diligently
Seek connection to God/higher self through prayer, devotion, or sacred study
Develop 'seer' vision: Look beyond surface appearances to deeper spiritual truths
Consider the eternal/bigger picture perspective on daily problems

Disciplined Excellence

Inspired by All Heroes

Progress 0%
Daily study of primary texts from philosophy, spirituality, or your field
Deliberately choose difficult tasks that build character
Stand by your principles even when it's socially uncomfortable
Integrate learning: How do today's insights change your worldview?

How to Use This Framework

Daily/Weekly Practice:

  • • Check boxes as you complete each practice
  • • Aim for 1-2 practices per day from different areas
  • • Reset weekly to track fresh progress
  • • Focus on consistency over perfection

Sync Across Devices:

  • • Use "Export Progress" to save your weekly progress
  • • Save the downloaded file to iCloud/cloud storage
  • • Use "Import Progress" on other devices to sync up
  • • Export at week's end to keep a record of your journey

Integration Reflection:

Weekly: How did these practices help you access deeper truth or see situations differently?

Monthly: Which hero's approach served you best this month and why? What intuitive insights emerged?

Quarterly: How has your connection to higher wisdom evolved? What new spiritual understanding is emerging?
