ⓘ Quick Reference

Mood Emojis

Click an emoji to insert it at your cursor in the note field. During comment editing, it inserts at the cursor there too.

😊Happy / good session
😤Frustrated — pushed through anyway
😬Nervous / uncertain
🤔Thoughtful / questioning
💪Strong effort

🔥 Streak

🔥Appears when you've logged entries on consecutive calendar days. Shows the current run — e.g. 🔥 3d means 3 days in a row. Miss a day and it resets.

📌 Pin

📌Click to pin a tally to the top. Ctrl+click pins with hot pink glow instead of cyan.

12W 3L

🃏Shown on a collapsed card — a quick score summary. W = wins (positive entries), L = losses (negative entries). Simple counter tallies show as 12/20 or just 12.

📅 Deadline

📅Optional. Set when adding or editing a tally. Shows days remaining as a pill in the card header — green = plenty of time, amber = 7 days or less, red = overdue. When a count target is also set, a pace line appears below the progress bar showing whether you're ahead, on track, or behind.
